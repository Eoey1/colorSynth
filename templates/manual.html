colourSynth - Manual

Play the synth with your computer keyboard

- Letters a to k play the white notes
- Letters w e t y and u play the black notes

Change octave with the ↑ ↓ arrow keys and pan the output by holding down
the ← or → arrow keys

Turn the delay on and off by pressing Spacebar

You can select which waveforms you want to use by clicking on the button
next to the image of each waveform type, or with number keys 1 - 4

Use the faders to adjust the levels of each waveform by dragging them
up or down with your mouse

Use the noise generator to fade in different kinds of noise, the colour of the
waveform corresponds to the type of noise being produced

To program the sequencer you always start by clicking in the box corresponding to the step of
the sequence you want to activate, then you can choose which note you want by pressing a key
on the keyboard using your mouse or playing a note using your computer keyboard

You can even just use the arrow keys to move up and down in semitones and left and right along the sequence
